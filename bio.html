About the Team

FURban Outfitters wouldn't be this amazing without these spectacular creators:

Sam Baker


Sam was born and raised on an Indian Reservation in a remote part of New Mexico. Instead of going to school, he spent most of his time being one with nature and watching reruns of Project Runway.

Hayley Landsberg


Hayley was raised by a wolf pack outside of Nebraska. She didn't learn english until she accidently got lost and ended up working in a sweatshop outside of NYC.

Glen Vollmer


Glen was adopted and grew up in Hong Kong. He is a bit of a recluse, so we don't know too much of his backstory, but it is rumored that he was a male model in a Tibetan village for many years.

Patrick Leon


Patrick grew up in a remote village in Alaska. He was the youngest of his 17 siblings, so all of his hand-me-down pants were always very worn and tattered. Because of this, he made it his life mission to make sure everyone had a good pair of pants in life. He took it a little too far when he decided to wrestle a bear into some skinny jeans... But to be fair, the bear looked great in those jeans!
